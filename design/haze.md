# Haze — The Playground Design System

Bauhaus-inspired geometric playground. Neon pink `#ff2d95` against complementary vibrant greens — mint `#00ff88`, teal `#00e5ff`, warm yellow `#ffb830`. Sharp corners, asymmetric shadows, bold type.

## Palette

| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| `--color-primary` | `#ff2d95` | Neon pink — buttons, borders, accents |
| `--color-accent` | `#39ff8c` | Mint green — hover states |
| `--color-emerald` | `#00ff88` | Neon green — complementary |
| `--color-amber` | `#ffb830` | Yellow pop |
| `--color-foreground` | `#fce4ec` | Text |

## Background

Floating geometric circles and ellipses drift across a deep purple-black canvas. A 20s `bauhaus-float` animation. No particles — geometry IS the decoration.

## Shape

| Token | Value | Effect |
|-------|-------|--------|
| `--radius` | `0px` | Sharp corners — rejects ornament |
| Cards | 3px solid border, 6px offset shadow | Cut paper aesthetic |
| Buttons | Color-flip on hover | Pink → green |

## Type

Solid neon pink, weight 900, uppercase, wide tracking. Bold geometric voice.

## Character

**Avant-garde, playful, primary.** Pink/green confrontation. Thick borders, offset shadows. Complement flip on hover. Nothing subtle — pure Bauhaus energy.
